Who are we?

We are a small team of environmentally cautious researchers. We have all been working together on different eco projects but now it was time to do something big, no, huge. This is our biggest, most ambitious project to date, and we're doing this, because it works.

What do we do?

We make houses. Admitedly with a bit of a twist. Through the years, there has been made a lot of houses, but none like ours. Our houses are self-sustainable and produce their own energy. Therefore we place them near rivers for watermills. They are often positioned in the low as well, making it easier to equip them with terra pumps and lastly we have solar cells anywhere we can put them. Our houses have back roofs, to help heat up the house, and big windows to prevent it from getting too warm. That's how you adjust the temperature.

Of course our houses aren't for everyone. It's a tiny house, some people prefer mansions, but here, you produce all the energy, for you and your house. You're independant, you're free.

Why are we doing this?

The climate and the environment matters. not because we are gonna kill the planet, but we're gonna kill ourselves. We need to think smarter about this. We might have subverted the past climate crisis, but that doesn't mean we can't be touched, and it doesn't mean, that a new one won't come around any time. So the solution starts now, we can't wait for the next breaking point. Action should start now.

Why should you choose us?
